	Mr. Charles Bradlaugh, the secularist, and Mrs. Annie Besant, a free-thought advocate, have been charged at the Guildhall, London, with selling a pamphlet entitled “Fruits of Philosophy,” and alleged to be immoral. Mr. Bradlaugh denies the immoral character of the book, which is said to deal with the over-population questions. The case is adjourned till the 17th inst.
