Mr. Bradlaugh and Mrs. Besant were present on Sunday evening at a lecture delivered at the North London secular society, in order to enroll members into the “Malthusian” League, the object of which are “to spread among the people a knowledge of the laws of population and of its practical application; to agitate for the abolition of all penalties on the public discussion of population question ; and to obtain such a statutory definition as shall render it impossible in the future to bring within the scope of the common law, as a misdemeanour, the publication of works dealing with this question,” The hall was crowded, and a number of person were enrolled as members.
