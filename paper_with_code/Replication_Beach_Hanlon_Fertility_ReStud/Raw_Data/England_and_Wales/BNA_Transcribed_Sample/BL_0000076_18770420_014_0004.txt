THE hearing of the charge against MR. BRADLAUGH and Mrs. BESANT was resumed yesterday, at the Guildhall, London. ALD. FIGGINS refused to admit medical opinion as evidence, and Mr. Bradlaugh resumed his speech, comparing passages in his pamphlet with other works in common circulation. Mrs. Besant also made a statement in defence. The defendants were bound over to appear at the Central Criminal Court on the 7th May.
