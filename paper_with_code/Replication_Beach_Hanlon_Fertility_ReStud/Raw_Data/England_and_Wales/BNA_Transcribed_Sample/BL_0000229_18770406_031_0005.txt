MR. CHARLES BRADLAUGH ND MRS. ANNIE BESANT
At the Guildhall Police-court, London, yesterday, Mr. Charles Bradlaugh, the well-known lecturer,  and Mrs. Annie Besant, journalist, another Free-thought advocate, were charged whit selling a work entitled ‘’Fruits of Philosophy; an Essay on the Population Question, by Charles Knowlton, M. D, “which, it is alleged, is of an immoral character. Mr. Bradlaugh said he should have forty witnesses to call, and asked for a long adjournment. He had given notice to the police authorities of his intention to sell the work. Alderman Figgins accepted bail in £200 each, until the 17th inst.
